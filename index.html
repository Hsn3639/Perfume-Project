When you buy a mixed basket, your real cost isn't the price of any single bottle — it's the average across every unit. Spread your total cost evenly and that average drops low, which hands you the freedom to price simply, double your money, and control your margin line by line.

The playbook

Real strategies retailers use

Keystone pricing

The classic "double your money": set retail at 2× cost (a 100% markup / 50% margin). Simple, fast, and the baseline most retail runs on.

Blended / portfolio margin

Price off your average cost, not each bottle. Cheap lines subsidise premium ones, so a single clean shelf price still profits across the whole basket.

Good–Better–Best laddering

Sell the same stock at three price points (entry, core, premium). You capture budget and big spenders and lift your average selling price.

Loss leaders & anchors

Price a few recognisable names low to pull traffic; let high-margin oriental brands and a premium "anchor" do the earning.

Inventory turnover

A low average cost sells faster. Reinvest each cycle's profit into the next order — turning stock 3–4× a year compounds returns far past a single markup.

Bundling

Group bottles into gift sets or "build-your-3" offers to raise average order value and move slower lines at a healthy blended margin.

Frequently asked

Who can order, and where do you deliver?

We supply registered retailers, pharmacies, concept stores and online resellers based in Malta and Italy. A valid business / VAT registration is required to open a trade account.

Why is there a 3–5 week lead time?

Orders are assembled to demand and quality-checked before despatch. The 3–5 week window covers stock consolidation, documentation and delivery booking from confirmed payment.

What is the minimum order?

€1,500 net per order, with a minimum of 7 units per reference. There is no maximum — volume pricing is available on request for large programmes.

Are the perfumes authentic?

Yes. Every reference is genuine, factory-sealed and carries a verifiable EAN barcode, which is listed beside each product.

Do prices include VAT and delivery?

No. All prices are per-unit wholesale, exclusive of VAT. Delivery is not included — it is calculated separately. Intra-EU VAT rules apply within Malta and Italy, and valid VAT-registered businesses may qualify for reverse-charge treatment subject to documentation.

How is delivery charged?

Delivery is quoted separately for each order once it's confirmed, based on your destination in Malta or Italy, the order volume and weight. The total you see in the order panel is for goods only; we add the delivery quote to your proforma invoice before payment.

How do I submit my order?

Build your order in the catalogue, then send it by email or WhatsApp directly from the order panel, or download a CSV to attach to your purchase order.
